How To Make Lasagna

Good italian dish with noodles and sauce and meat and layers

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cream cheese

Directions

  1. Buy Ingredients
  2. preheat oven to 400 degress F and cook meat for 1 hour
  3. Soften noodles in water and layer noodles then meat then cream cheese
  4. Repeat until there is a layer of noodles on top and bottom
  5. Cook lasanga for 350 degrees F for 30 mintues or until noodles are cooked
